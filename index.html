03 — The Culture

COMMUNITY & COMPLIANCE

◈
What is Solventless?
Rosin is extracted using only heat and pressure — no butane, propane, or chemical solvents. The result is a pure, terpene-intact concentrate that reflects the exact genetic profile of the plant. Ice water hash takes it further: trichomes separated in ice water, freeze-dried, then pressed.
❄
The 805 Terroir
Ventura, Santa Barbara, and San Luis Obispo counties form one of California's most distinct cannabis-growing microclimates. Pacific marine influence, diurnal temperature swings, and coastal humidity create terpene expressions that can't be replicated inland. The 805 isn't just an area code — it's a denomination of origin.
⌧
Rosin Textures Explained
Fresh Press: immediate post-press, fluid consistency, maximum terpene volatility. Cold Cure: sealed and held at low temp, transforms into a saucy stable consistency. Budder: whipped to incorporate air, creating a lighter creamy texture. Each serves a different rig setup and temperature preference.
